The visual style of the galaxy is inspired by nebulas and star clusters. As mentioned in the [[galaxy-introduction]], we want a borderless, glowing effect for nodes.

This adheres to our broader [[design-principles]] while adding a futuristic touch. We use additive blending to achieve that ethereal glow.
